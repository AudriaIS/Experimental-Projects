According to the legend, which has been passed down to us through the wise and all-knowing Princess Analise. Dragon mothers search the world over for the finest of vessels to place their baby dragons. Only when held by someone they deem equal in heart and mind will this baby dragon hatch from its vessel. Your dragon egg may not hatch for centuries. Continue to believe and work on keeping your heart and mind pure.
